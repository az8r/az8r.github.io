Price

    Pricing Method


Our products will be accordingly regulated upon a cost based pricing system where it subsidises the costs to the business, we have a current markup of 145%. We specifically set this as the markup price lower than most tech companies to entise consumers to spend money of the product but just high enough that we will be able to make a profit within the end of the year.


    Positioning


We know we are not the top technological business within our field and that’s why we have set our markup / cost of product to be marginally lower than some of our competitors as we are able to show our product is not highly expensive but reliable and within the higher quality tier.
