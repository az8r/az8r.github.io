Business Information

Name: Rain. Rain is a private, online, interactive, forum based website that helps cultivate and expand peoples knowledge of coding and teamwork. It a similar platform to that of reddit were business's or charities can post job threads where the community can participate within to create the desired program, website, etc... Rain however cannot be accessed normally through a browser link as it is a standalone server. To access this server you will have to buy the Rain Modem Box, this will allow you to participate within the posted projects. This concept allows people around the globe to come together to partipate within the creation of a new software or coding based application. Your ability to code and knowledge of different coding languages will determine the projects you will be able to participate in. If you reach title 'Master' you will be able to submit projects for the community to participate in, once accepted by the moderators that it.




Prime Function

Rain’s prime function is to help furthher develop individuals understanding of programming and teamwork skills through online based projects set out by company's, charities and high ranking members of the community. We want our customers to become proud of their creation knowing that a consumer somewhere is hopefully enjoying using a product they ahve made.




Location

Currently Rain is an online based system that operates from within the servers in our factory. Rain does not have a physical store to buy the product as thhis will be done online. We do have manufacturing location in 21 Binney Rd, Kings Park NSW. This building has a floor area of 2,626m2, land area of 4,318m2. There is an onsite allowance of 36 car spaces. For a current yearly rental it is $304k plus GST. For the factory employee’s there is conveniently placed forms of transportation such as bus and train which are both within a kilometer range of the complex.
