Legal Requirements



    Rain pty ltd has various legal requirements that concern the business's and the employee's wellbeing when producing and conducting business, such as


  • Occupational Safety and Health

    • This law / legal requirement places a general duty of care over my employee's so that, I the employer provide information, instruction, training to, and supervision as necessary to enable them to perform their work in such a manner that they are not exposed to hazards.

  • First aid facilities / First aid in the workplace

    • Adopting this legal requirement helps reduce the risk of further employee injury. This act requires the business to provide immediate and effective first aid to workers or others who have been injured or become ill at the workplace may reduce the severity of the injury or illness and promote recovery. In some cases it could mean the difference between life and death. This can be achieved by having various accessible first aid kits in the walkway and all facilities / rooms of the complex.

  • Regulation and registration of business name

    • Registering the business's name will stop anyone from creating a similar company and can help avoid the risk of slanderous comments if another business will the same name and intention comes under legality issues. It is a legal obligation of most jurisdictions and ensures that a business is operating under the lawful obligations of its control.

  • Workplace health and safety

    • The WHS Act places the primary health and safety duty on a person conducting a business or undertaking (PCBU). The PCBU must ensure, so far as is reasonably practicable, the health and safety of workers at the workplace. Duties are also placed on officers of a PCBU, workers and other persons at a workplace.

  • Drug Testing

    • This legality requirement is set in place as to stop co workers in dangering themselves within the workplace if they are under the influence of drugs or harmful substances
